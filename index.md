## Varsha Sharma

AI researcher in healthcare with over 8 years of experience at TCS Research, working on machine learning systems for biomedical signal analysis, wearable sensing, and real-world health applications. Since October 2025, I have been working as a researcher at the The University of Osaka, where I will begin my PhD in April 2026.

My work focuses on building end-to-end AI systems for healthcare, spanning:
- physiological signal analysis (ECG, cardiovascular monitoring)
- wearable sensing and on-device intelligence (TinyML)
- digital biomarkers from non-invasive sensing (VOC-based analysis)
- clinical prediction using heterogeneous healthcare data
